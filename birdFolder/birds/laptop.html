A Little Birdie

The four of you are bird watchers and collecters of your favourite species...

The scarlet tanager,

The bluejay,

The tree swallow,

and the meadowlark.

Tilt your phone to move your binoculars & catch the birds.

But don't venture through the river or over the fence!

See the birds you've collected on your phone, and use the buttons to trade with fellow birdwatchers!

To win, collect all four of your favourite species!
